# SchoolOS-Global

SchoolOS-Global is a centralized platform designed to modernize school operations by replacing traditional pen-and-paper methods. It provides a complete digital ecosystem that empowers administrators and educators to seamlessly manage students, staff, classes, and day-to-day administrative tasks from a single, unified interface.

## Features

- **Student Management:** Efficiently track enrollments, demographics, and student records.
- **Teacher Management:** Organize staff profiles, schedules, and assignments.
- **Class Management:** Streamline the creation and organization of classes and subjects.
- **Attendance Management:** Quickly record and monitor daily attendance for students and staff.
- **Grade Management:** Securely input, calculate, and store student academic performance.
- **Fee Management:** Simplify tuition tracking, invoicing, and payment processing.
- **Report Card Generation:** Automatically generate comprehensive and customizable academic reports.
- **Library Management:** Manage book inventories, checkouts, and student reading histories.
